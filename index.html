Student Social Learning System

New Learning Environment System To help collaboration between Parent, Teacher and Students

Our goal is to change the classroom experience by developing an open web-based learning management system which will closely monitor a students activity throughout the year providing customized and direct metrics to teachers and parents. We will provide an engaging platform for students to interact socially, review lecture material and assignments. Teachers will be able to assess student performance through our unique grading scheme based on information we get from the student. Parents will be provided with a roadmap/outline of children's courses, analyze performances, and communicate with teachers. Unlike our competitors, who focus on providing content, we want to focus on the interactive experience. How are students interacting with one another? How are they performing throughout the year? How can a teacher use historical data for a student to analyze his/her performance over the years?
